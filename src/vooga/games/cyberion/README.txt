Authors: Hao He, Scott Winkleman, Vitor Olivier

Description: 
Cyberion is a game in which a player ship flies through space 
and has to destroy enemy ships while avoiding their shots in 
order to avoid destruction.

Instructions:
Use arrow keys to move and F to fire.
Red square are bonuses which will increase the player's weapon
power when they collide.
Avoid enemies and their shots at all costs because they will
decrease your life and shot power upon collision.
